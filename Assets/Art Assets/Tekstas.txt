Aitvaras is a creature from Lithuanian mythology. It collects wealth, and can bring both good and bad luck to its owner. What you get will depend on you player.

This a project was made by students for Klaipeda State University of Applied Sciences (KVK) as a Agile exercice.

Autors: Artûras Gailius, Algirdas Straukas, Rokas Aukðtakis, Neda Mikelionytë, Deividas Palðauskas.

Klaipëda, 2019.

Orange: #ff9a15 ( R.255; G.154; B.21);
Red: #ff5f15 (255, 95, 21);
Yellow: #ffd415 (255, 212, 21);
